In SCP terminology, "Phantom" is typically used as a cover designation or codename for a highly classified strike team or black-ops unit within the Foundation.

Because the Foundation compartmentalizes information, the real name and activities of some teams are redacted or concealed, and they are referred to with placeholder terms like "Phantom," "Specter," "Null," etc. This lets the Foundation talk about the existence of the unit without disclosing what it actually does.

Why it exists

A Phantom team is usually created for one of the following reasons:

Purpose Description
Extreme secrecy. The team handles missions too sensitive, dangerous, or ethically questionable to be logged under normal task force parameters.
Anomalous operations. They may use or interact with anomalies in ways that are not sanctioned for normal Foundation units.
Off-the-books actions. The team may perform unauthorized containment, neutralization, or intelligence gathering that can't be traced back to the Foundation.
Internal contingency Sometimes Phantom teams exist to monitor or eliminate other Foundation personnel should they become compromised.

What "Phantom" means in-universe

The term does not refer to a specific SCP.

It's more like a codename for a hidden unit.

Their members, mission reports, and clearance are all classified.

A - CoE 0:1 - Ethics Hierarchy

Note, this is only for Ethical actions(i.e What is ethical and what is not, this is not for ordering around subjects)

The Hierarchy goes as follows:

Class-A Personnel - <Executive>

Class-A Personnel >> Intelligence Personnel, Executives, O5s and The Administrator.

Classifications are assigned to personnel based on their proximity to potentially dangerous anomalous objects, entities, or phenomena.

Class A personnel are those deemed essential to Foundation strategic operations, and are not allowed direct access to anomalies under any circumstances. When circumstances require Class A personnel to be in direct proximity to such anomalies (such as in the case of facilities housing containment units), Class A personnel are not allowed access to the areas of the facility containing such anomalies and are to be kept in secured areas at all times. In the case of an emergency, Class A personnel are to be immediately evacuated to a designated and secure off-site location. O5 Council members are always Class A personnel.

Class-B Personnel - <Civil>

Class-B Personnel >> Administrative Department, Internal Tribunal Department, Ethics Committee

Class B personnel are those deemed essential to local Foundation operations, and may only be granted access to objects, entities, and anomalies that have passed quarantine and have been cleared of any potential mind-affecting effects or memetic agents. In the event of a containment breach or hostile action against a Foundation facility, Class B personnel are to be evacuated to a designated, secure off-site location as soon as possible.

Class-C Personnel - <Combative Division>

Class-C Personnel >> Combative Personnel, Combative High Ranks

Class C personnel are personnel with direct access to most anomalies not deemed strictly hostile or dangerous. Class C personnel that have had direct contact with potentially mind-affecting or memetic properties may be subject to mandatory quarantine and psychiatric evaluation as deemed necessary by security personnel. In the event of a containment breach or hostile action against a Foundation facility, non-combatant Class C personnel are to either report to secure lock-down areas or evacuated at the discretion of on-site security personnel in the case of a site-wide breach or other catastrophic event.

Class-D Personnel - <Foundation Personnel>

Class-D Personnel >> Classification-Disposable, D-Class, Security Department, Scientific Department, Medical Department, Manufacturing Department, Engineering and Technical

Class D is a classification given to those deemed non-essential to any of the Foundations operations and hence forth were deemed "Disposable-Personnel" they have access to be around any anomalies for research or security purposes as the foundation losing them is deemed expendable in the case of a breach they are to be escorted to a Breach shelter by security personnel. Amnestics treatment is recommended for all site-personnel and is absolutely required for any combative personnel below level 4 after the containment breach. (Note: This excluded any and all research personnel unless they saw something they shouldn't have.)

Class-E Personnel - <No Rights Entitled>

Class-E Personnel >> Classification-Epilepsy, E-Class

Class E is a provisional classification applied to field agents and containment personnel that have been exposed to potentially dangerous effects during the course of securing and establishing initial containment over a newly-designated anomalous object, entity, or phenomenon. Class E personnel are to be quarantined as soon as possible, monitored, and screened for potentially harmful changes in behavior, personality, or physiology, and may only return to duty after being fully debriefed and cleared by psychiatric and medical staff.
